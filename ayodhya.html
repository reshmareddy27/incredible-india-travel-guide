Ayodhya: The Sacred Birthplace of Lord Rama

Spiritual Significance

Ayodhya is revered as the birthplace of Lord Rama. It holds immense significance in Hindu mythology and is one of the seven sacred cities. The Ram Janmabhoomi temple stands as a symbol of devotion and heritage.
