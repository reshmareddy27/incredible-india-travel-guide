Dwaraka - Travel Information

The Sacred Kingdom of Lord Krishna

📜 Spiritual Significance

Dwaraka is one of the Char Dham pilgrimage sites and was once the capital of Lord Krishna's kingdom. It holds immense religious importance in Hinduism.

📍 Location

Dwarka, Devbhoomi Dwarka District, Gujarat, India

🗺 Google Maps

🚌 How to Reach

  • ✈ By Air: Nearest airport is Jamnagar (137 km away)
  • 🚆 By Train: Dwarka Railway Station is well-connected
  • 🚌 By Road: Regular buses from major cities in Gujarat

🕒 Temple Timings

6:30 AM – 1:00 PM and 5:00 PM – 9:30 PM

📅 Best Time to Visit

October to March for pleasant weather and festivals

🎉 Major Festivals

  • Janmashtami
  • Diwali
  • Holi

💡 Tips for Visitors

  • Wear modest clothing while visiting temples
  • Remove footwear before entering temple premises
  • Carry cash for offerings and local shopping
  • Plan early morning visits to avoid rush

🏞 Nearby Attractions

  • Bet Dwarka Island
  • Rukmini Devi Temple
  • Gomti Ghat
  • Dwarka Beach

🌟 Why Visit Dwaraka?

  • Explore one of the Char Dham and Sapta Puri pilgrimage sites
  • Experience the spiritual essence of Lord Krishna’s legendary kingdom
  • Admire the majestic Dwarkadhish Temple and sacred Gomti Ghat
  • Enjoy the peaceful blend of divine history and coastal beauty
