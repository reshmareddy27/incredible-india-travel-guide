Tirupati Travel Info

Tirupati:Abode of the Lord of Seven Hills

📍 Location

Tirupati is located in the Chittoor district of Andhra Pradesh, India, at the foothills of the Tirumala Hills.

🗺 Google Maps

🚆 How to Reach

  • By Air: Tirupati Airport is 15 km from the city.
  • By Train: Tirupati Railway Station is well-connected to major cities.
  • By Road: Frequent buses and taxis are available from nearby cities like Chennai, Bengaluru, and Hyderabad.

🛕 Temple Timings

The temple is open from 3:00 AM to 1:30 AM (next day), with multiple darshan slots available throughout the day.

🌤 Best Time to Visit

October to March is ideal due to pleasant weather and fewer crowds.

🎉 Major Festivals

  • Brahmotsavam
  • Vaikunta Ekadasi
  • Rathasapthami

💡 Tips for Visitors

  • Book darshan tickets in advance on the official TTD website.
  • Follow the temple dress code (traditional attire).
  • Free meals and accommodation are provided by TTD.
  • Start early in the morning to avoid queues.

🧭 Nearby Attractions

  • Kapila Theertham
  • Sri Padmavathi Ammavari Temple
  • Chandragiri Fort

🌟 Why Visit Tirupati?

  • To seek the blessings of Lord Venkateswara
  • To be part of one of the richest and most spiritual temples in the world
  • To witness the devotion of millions of pilgrims
  • To enjoy the natural beauty of the Tirumala Hills
