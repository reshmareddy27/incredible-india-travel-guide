Puri Jagannath

A Sacred Pilgrimage in Odisha

🛕Spiritual Significance

Puri is one of the Char Dham pilgrimage sites and is especially revered for the Jagannath Temple dedicated to Lord Jagannath (a form of Krishna). The temple is known for the annual Rath Yatra, during which the deities are paraded in massive chariots.

📍Location

Puri, Odisha, India

🚆How to Reach

  • By Air: Nearest airport is Biju Patnaik International Airport (60 km from Puri).
  • By Train: Puri Railway Station is well connected to major cities in India.
  • By Road: Buses and taxis are easily available from Bhubaneswar and nearby towns.

🛕Temple Timings

5:00 AM to 11:30 PM (timings may vary during festivals)

🌤Best Time to Visit

October to March is ideal for pleasant weather. Visit during the Rath Yatra (June-July) for a unique experience.

🎉Major Festivals

  • Rath Yatra
  • Snana Yatra
  • Chandan Yatra

💡Tips for Visitors

  • Non-Hindus are not allowed inside the main temple but can view it from outside the gate.
  • Photography is strictly prohibited inside the temple.
  • Maintain modest attire and respect religious customs.

🧭Nearby Attractions

  • Konark Sun Temple
  • Puri Beach
  • Chilika Lake

🌟Why Visit Puri Jagannath?

Visiting Puri is not just a religious experience, but also a chance to witness Odisha’s rich culture and history. The grand Rath Yatra draws millions of pilgrims and tourists, showcasing the deep devotion and ancient traditions of India.
