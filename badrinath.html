Badrinath - Travel Info

🌄 A Temple of Rich History and Culture.

🕉 Spiritual Significance

Badrinath is a sacred pilgrimage site dedicated to Lord Vishnu and is part of both the Char Dham and Chota Char Dham circuits.

📍 Location

Chamoli district, Uttarakhand, India

🚗 How to Reach

  • By Road: NH7 via Rishikesh or Joshimath
  • By Train: Nearest station – Rishikesh (295 km)
  • By Air: Jolly Grant Airport, Dehradun (310 km)

🕒 Temple Timings

Morning: 4:30 AM – 1:00 PM
Evening: 4:00 PM – 9:00 PM

📅 Best Time to Visit

May to October (closed during winter due to snow)

🎉 Major Festivals

  • Badri-Kedar Utsav
  • Mata Murti Ka Mela

💡 Tips for Visitors

  • Carry warm clothes year-round
  • High altitude – allow time to acclimate
  • Book transport and hotels in advance

🏞 Nearby Attractions

  • Mana Village
  • Tapt Kund
  • Vasudhara Falls
  • Charanpaduka

🌟 Why Visit Badrinath?

  • One of the Char Dham – a sacred Hindu pilgrimage site
  • Dedicated to Lord Vishnu in his Badrinarayan form
  • Surrounded by the majestic Himalayas and Alaknanda River
  • Spiritual legends of Adi Shankaracharya and the Pandavas
  • Home to Tapt Kund – a natural thermal spring with healing properties
  • Believed to purify the soul and lead toward moksha (liberation)
